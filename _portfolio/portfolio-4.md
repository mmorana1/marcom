The OWASP AI Testing Guide is designed to help security leaders, practitioners, and developers understand and manage the security risks associated with modern AI systems. It offers practical guidance on testing AI applications, applying threat modeling techniques, and leveraging relevant OWASP resources. The guide aims to support organizations in systematically evaluating and securing AI-driven systems, including applications built with large language models (LLMs) and generative AI technologies.
